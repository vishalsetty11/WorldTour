CANADIAN MOUNTAINS

Canada is a country of extreme beauty, be it the ocean vistas, the coasts or the vast expanses of the prairies. You can never get bored of the surprises that this country has to offer. However, nothing can beat the gorgeousness of Canada mountains. This article deals with letting you know about the top 10 Canada mountains to climb and discusses the features which make them as amazing as they are. So, if you're a person who is mad about mountains and you're planning your mountaineering trip to Canada, here you go. The best time to go climbing in the Cannadian mountains is between June to September.

Expenses break down

⦿ Airfare: 1400 CAD per person so 2800 CAD for two of you. (Round trip from Indian metropolitan cities)

⦿ Accommodation: You can stay in AirBnB or at a hotel for an average cost of 110 CAD per night with 3 star ratings.

⦿ Food and Drinks: Food is not cheap if you plan to eat out whether Indian or Canadian. You can expect to spend 20 CAD per day for basic food options like McDonalds, KFC etc or buy a loaf of bread, milk and eggs.

⦿ Attractions and Daily Commute: 120 CAD a day.

Total Expenses:

Approximately should be 5700 CAD ~ 3.45 lakhs INR.
